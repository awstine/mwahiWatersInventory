Our Impact Areas

Infrastructure (WASH)

  • Borehole Rehabilitation

    Restoring boreholes to optimal functioning by cleaning, repairing, and replacing parts for reliable clean water.

  • Spring Protection

    Safeguarding natural water sources by diverting surface runoff and constructing protective structures.

  • Pipeline Installation & Solarization

    Construction of pipelines feeding into reservoirs via gravitational flow and solar-powered systems.

  • Communal Water Points

    Constructed to reduce queuing time and the distance of water points to households.

Community & Health

  • Public Health

    Promoting community well-being through targeted public health programs. We've educated over 5,000 people to foster positive social behavior change and provided treatment for jigger infestations to more than 2,000 children and community members.

  • School WASH

    Improving learning environments with safe water, hygiene facilities, and wash clubs.

  • Capacity Building

    Collaborating with communities to improve their skills, abilities, and available resources.

  • VSLA (Village Savings & Loans)

    Empowering the community to overcome social and financial barriers through our Village Savings & Loans initiative, which supports 10 groups of 30 members each.
